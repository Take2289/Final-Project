About Us

Build A Bank began in August 2020 and was presented by Money Management Solutions . Starting in Philadelphia, Pa. This program was designed to give better insight into a person's finances and budget potential. The CEO Ty Davidson, decided that this could be a beneficial tool that people can utilize to keep up with their monthly or yearly expenses. In harsh and difficult times it our task to help people take a closer look at their budget and savings. We work together to assist customers and produce results that we are proud of for people that we believe in. We present this system not only as an online tool, but we hope to have this tool function implemented into daily life. ?

It is our goal and mission to help society produced the most desired results when it comes to saving and finances. Our team is here and is on standby ready to assist customers that may have any further questions. We also provide addition services free of charge, that includes tips and best practices to assure our customers end up in the best financial circumstances possible. We assure all of our customers will receive the best service. We hope to play a huge role and be a integral part of your money management journey.
